About Entsha Logistics

For over three decades, Entsha Logistics has been the backbone of South Africa's fresh produce supply chain.

Established in 1988, we've built our reputation on reliability, expertise, and deep understanding of the unique requirements of perishable goods transportation.

Our subsidiary, Johnson Vervoer, extends our capabilities with specialized warehousing services and comprehensive maintenance solutions.

Our Journey Since 1988

Three decades of excellence in South African logistics

1988 - Foundation

Entsha Logistics was established with a focus on fresh produce transportation, recognizing the unique needs of this vital industry.

1995 - Expansion

Expanded our fleet and services to cover more regions across South Africa, building a reputation for reliability.

2005 - Johnson Vervoer

Launched our subsidiary Johnson Vervoer to provide specialized warehousing and maintenance services.

2015 - B-BBEE Recognition

Achieved B-BBEE Level 4 Contributor status, demonstrating our commitment to economic transformation.

2023 - Digital Transformation

Embarking on our digital journey to serve clients better in the modern era while maintaining our traditional values of reliability and excellence.
